Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

At Abious Foundation, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you use our website, member portal, and Chrome extension.

📋 Quick Summary
We collect only necessary information, never sell your data, and implement strong security measures to protect your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Account Credentials: Username, encrypted password
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, member details
  • Communication Data: Messages you send us, feedback

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, features used
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage statistics for improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

🔐 Account Management

Create and manage your member account

📬 Communications

Send updates, newsletters, and respond to inquiries

📊 Service Improvement

Analyze usage to improve our services

🛡️ Security

Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

3.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

Abious Foundation does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

3.2 When We May Share Data

Recipient Purpose Data Shared
Service Providers Hosting, analytics, email delivery Limited to necessary functions
Legal Authorities Compliance with law enforcement When legally required
Emergency Services Health/safety emergencies Only when necessary

4. Data Storage & Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

  • Primary Database: MongoDB Atlas (Cloud hosting with data centers)
  • Backup Storage: Encrypted cloud backups
  • Extension Data: Local browser storage (not synced to cloud)

4.2 Security Measures

  • TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee training on data protection
  • Access controls and authentication requirements

5. Your Rights

Under applicable privacy laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("Right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing for certain purposes
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

6.1 Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Core functionality, login sessions Session
Analytics Cookies Understand website usage Up to 1 year
Preference Cookies Remember your settings Up to 1 year

6.2 Managing Cookies

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that some features may not work properly without cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Browser Extension Privacy

Our Chrome/Edge extension (Abious Foundation) has the following privacy practices:

  • Local Storage: Extension settings stored locally in your browser
  • No Cloud Sync: Personal data is NOT synced to cloud services
  • Limited Permissions: Only requests necessary permissions for functionality
  • No Tracking: Does not track browsing history or collect analytics
  • Open Source: Extension code can be reviewed for transparency

9. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • MongoDB Atlas: Database hosting - Privacy Policy
  • Render: Cloud hosting - Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts: Typography - Privacy Policy

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary:

  • Active Accounts: Retained while account is active
  • Inactive Accounts: Data retained for 2 years after last login
  • Deleted Accounts: Data permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Backup Data: Automatically purged after retention period

11. International Data Transfers

Your data may be stored and processed in servers outside your country. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including Standard Contractual Clauses and adequacy decisions.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes

13. Contact Information

📬 Contact Us

If you have questions, want to exercise your rights, or need more information about our privacy practices:

Email: privacy@abiousfoundation.org
Subject Line: Privacy Inquiry

We will respond to your request within 30 days.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency in all our data practices. Thank you for trusting Abious Foundation with your information.
